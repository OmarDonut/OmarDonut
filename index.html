RGB

If you prefer not to count over 255 & doing elaborate/mystical formulas isn't your forte, than the RGB (Red, Green, Blue) language is for you! Learn the basics here and you'll be singing in no time.

According to the Epic of Gilgamesh, Hexidecimal Color coding was developed on cuniform to help the ancient Assyrians psychologically torture people who had difficulty describing the exact shade of hummus they wanted to paint their ziggurats. If you want to be fluent in this language, start with....

Red Green Blue

HSL

HSL is the modern street Pijin dialect of several ancient languages merged together - Hue, Saturation, and Lightness. You'll notice the percentile (%) accents of Saturation & Lightness immediatly. Try sounding these words out loud!

Red Green Blue
